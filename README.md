**Quantification of microplastic ecosystem disruption in world's #1 plastic-polluting river (Pasig River)**

## Pasig River Microplastic Research
**High School Science Club | 2026.02-19**

## 🎯 Research Objective
Quantification of waste composition & water quality degradation  
*Pasig River: 38,800 tons plastic/year (The Environment Journal, 2021)*

🔬 **High School Methods** (Field-Realistic)
- **Coffee filter (0.35mm)**: **34 plastic-like particles** (≥0.35mm)  
- **Smartphone 10x zoom + LED illumination** for counting
- **NIR Plastic Scanner app**: PE/PP spectral confirmation
- **Replicated 2018 Pasig study filtration protocol**

## 💡 **Engineering Solutions**
1. **Arduino IoT Smart Trap** ₱1,500 (Real-time monitoring)
2. **PET-degrading microbes** (75% degradation/6 weeks)
3. **"48% Plastic = 576kg"** Community Campaign
